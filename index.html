Constellus
The creators of Constellus believe:
  1. All human beings have equal worth, regardless of wealth, race, religion, affiliation, belief, class, or any other variable.
  2. The current order prevents the majority of human beings from being well.
Therefore:
  1. We advocate for mass, coordinated, collective action to affect change that will adjust the order to prioritize the majority.
  2. We are a community bound together by a common struggle, love, respect, trust, and, most importantly, hope.
  3. We welcome all human beings in search of dignity, love, fairness, and peace to join us.
